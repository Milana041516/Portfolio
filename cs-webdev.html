<Zima Drink Rebranding Project>

Project Overview: This project focused on rebranding the Zima drink, where I took on the role of front-end developer. We aimed to create a modernized brand identity and build a functional webpage that showcased the redesign.

Role & Contribution: I was responsible for the front-end development, coding the website based on the design provided by my partner. We used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build the entire site and incorporated Cinema 4D to create a 3D model of the Zima bottle and its packaging.

Process: My partner, Music, designed the website's layout and visuals while I worked on crafting the 3D bottle. She then created a promotional video, and I focused on coding the webpage, ensuring the integration of all elements. Together, we implemented our "Music Mixer" feature, initially created as a homework assignment, into the website. This project was particularly exciting because it marked our first attempt at combining 3D work and JavaScript. The hands-on experience was incredibly enriching and a great opportunity to explore new skills.

Challenge: One of the major challenges was the risk of misunderstanding during the collaboration, which could have led to missing the deadline. However, we learned to manage our time better and work more effectively together.

Tools used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Cinema 4D.

Final Result: Despite the initial challenges, we successfully delivered a fully functional and visually appealing website, along with a 3D-rendered model of the Zima bottle and its packaging.
